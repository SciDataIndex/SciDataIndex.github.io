SciDataIndex

An AI4Science platform for scientific data classification, grading, and evaluation

AI4Science Scientific Data Evaluation Framework

SciDataIndex is developed by the Shanghai AI Lab based on AI Agent technology. It targets AI4Science scenarios and builds a global framework for scientific data signal mining, classification, grading, and comprehensive evaluation. The platform continuously aggregates multi-source scientific data resources and performs quantitative assessment across openness, standardization, impact, and usability, supporting discovery of high-value research data and interdisciplinary innovation.
